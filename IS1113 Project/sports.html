Sports


- I attended dance and musical theatre lessons in the Firkin Crane between the ages of 3 until I was 17. Musicals and drama are passions of mine and I have partaken in many musical productions. Some of which include Beauty and the Beast, Alice in Wonderland, Les Miserables, Miss Saigon, Blood Brothers, CHICAGO, and many more. I now am a current member of the UCC Dance Society and attend weekly classes in the Mardyke.



- I played Gaelic Football and Camogie throughout primary and secondary school. I played with Brian Dillons GAA club as well as my school teams. I often go to watch Cork GAA matches also in the newly done up Pairc Ui Caoimh. Since going into college I have not played competively however I intend on joining the UCC GAA Club next semester.



- I enjoy going to the gym to exercise also. UCC students get free membership in the Mardyke Gym, which I get great use of.
